Thank you for shopping with us. We strive to ensure customer satisfaction with every purchase. Please read our Refund Policy carefully to understand your rights and our procedures regarding refunds.

Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund under the following conditions:

  • The product is defective, damaged, or not as described upon arrival
  • The wrong item was received
  • The product cannot function as intended due to a manufacturing issue

To be eligible for a refund:

  • The refund request must be submitted within 14 days of delivery
  • The product must be unused, unmodified, and returned in its original condition
  • The customer must provide a valid receipt or proof of purchase
  • The product must be returned with complete original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, cables, and labels

Refunds may be rejected if any required item or packaging is missing.

Non-Refundable Items

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Products damaged due to misuse, accidents, or unauthorized repairs
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Products returned without a valid receipt or incomplete original packaging
  • Missing accessories or components
  • Items marked as final sale or non-refundable (if applicable)
  • Requests submitted after the refund period has expired

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, please contact our customer support team and provide:

  • Your order number or receipt
  • A brief description of the issue
  • Clear photos or videos showing the condition of the product and packaging

Once your request is reviewed and approved, we will provide return instructions.

Return Shipping

  • Customers are responsible for the return shipping costs when sending the product back to us
  • Original shipping fees (if any) are non-refundable, unless the return is due to our error

We recommend using a trackable shipping method, as we are not responsible for items lost or damaged during return shipment.

Refund Processing Time

  • Refunds are processed after the returned item is received and inspected
  • Approved refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your account, depending on your payment provider

Exchange Policy

If you prefer an exchange instead of a refund (e.g. replacement for a defective item), please inform our customer support team when submitting your request. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

International Orders

For international orders:

  • Import duties, taxes, and customs fees are non-refundable
  • Customers are responsible for all return shipping costs and customs documentation
  • Refund amounts will exclude any fees charged by customs authorities

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Refund Policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact our customer support team through our website or support email.

We are committed to resolving issues fairly, transparently, and efficiently.
